Welcome!

A fan page for disco elysium to showcase artwork, blog posts, and have a hub for all things Disco Elysium.

Disco Elysium was created in 2019 as a game without combat and only dialog choices to guide your playthrough. There are several emotions that serve as the skills of the main character, Harry Du Bois. Due to the nature of the game, it comes off as more of a book than a video game detering some from playing it through to the end. Even if you have never played it I aim to summarize some of the most meaningful themes and philosophies of the game. I also encourage you even if you have never played video games to try it out.

  • "She thinks you are an idiot, sire."
  • "That's the spirit! Never forget: The whole world's a wooden house and you're a goddamn flamethrower."
  • "In the dark times, should the stars also go out?"
  • "That we continue to persist at all is a testament to our faith in one another."
